Roasted Asparagus With Parmensan

Roasted asparagus is a simple and delicious side dish that is perfect for any meal. It's easy to make and can be made ahead of time. It's also a great way to use up asparagus that is starting to get a little old.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch asparagus, trimmed
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Directions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Place asparagus in a single layer on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with salt and pepper. Toss to coat.
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until asparagus is tender.
  4. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese and serve immediately.
